Find your nearest COVID-19 testing station

How it works?

Please be responsible, and only reach your nearest COVID-19 testing station if you present the symptoms, or have been in contact with confirmed cases.

Search

Write the name of your city inside of the search box, and click the search button. You can also click in Stations on the top menu to view all the stations in the list.

Review

You can submit a review about your experience with the station visited, so more people can be informed about the current situation in the station (Traffic, Safety, etc.).

Choose

Once you click on the search button, a list of the nearest stations to your city (or in your city) will appear in the results page. Carefully choose the nearest one, and avoid direct contact with other drivers.

Update

If you know about a new testing station openning in your city, please open an issue ticket in our github repository so we can update our list of stations.

+200 stations and counting

We are contstantly updating our stations list as soon as a new station is open around the world.

Anyone can contribute

We are an open source project, you can always create a pull request, open an issue or help us with updating the list.

Stay Safe

Follow the advice of the World Health Organization, and your local authorities.
Don't take unncesary risks and stay at home. Think about your family and loved ones.
